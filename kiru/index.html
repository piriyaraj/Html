ATI Jaffna

Advanced Technological Institute, Jaffna (ATI-Jaffna) is one of the 11 institutes managed by SLIATE. The institute has been inaugurated in Jaffna peninsula in 1996. The main vision of the institute is to become the center of excellence in technological education in Northern Province. At present there are eight Higher National Diploma programmers including, Higher National Diploma in Engineering(civil, electrical & quantity surveying), Higher National Diploma in Management, Higher National Diploma in Accountancy, Higher National Diploma in English, Higher National diploma in Information Technology and Higher National Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management.
